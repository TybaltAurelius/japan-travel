## About This Travelogue

This site documents the travels of Sean and Mae throughout Japan, starting with their first trip in the Fall of 2023. Japanese culture, food, history, and geography are so meaningful to us, and we want to share our experiences and our love for this country and its people with everyone. Please enjoy and live vicariously through our pics and videos!
